How to connect your sheet

  1. Publish your sheet to the web: File → Share → Publish to the web.
  2. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL (the long string between /d/ and /edit).
  3. Update sheetId and sheetName in app.js.

Tip: keep the first row of your sheet as headers for a nice table layout.
